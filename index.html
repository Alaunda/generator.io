Hi! I'm Ally!

Because I'm your ally.
Pun definitely intended.

My goal here is to give and share kindess with you. So you can carry it with you throughout your day and possibly share this with others. You are always deserving of kindess and so are others. I respect you, your pronouns, and your existence as it is. So if anything I say causes you to feel offended or unheard, I apologize fully and I want you to know I am constantly seeking advice, critique and revision. So let's start sharing kindess together!



Need More Help?

I am so sorry if the words and phrases didn't give you the level of comfort and unity you need. I will always be here as a source of wellness. My words and phrases have and will always be an attempt of care to (and for) you. But sometimes, this on it's own may not be enough. If you need more, that's okay and completely normal! So here is a list of hotlines, self help, and outreaches to further assist you in you journey. Your are loved, you are love and I have so much faith in you. Thank you for stopping by!

  • Physical Emergency?! Please Dial: 911 (This will connect you wil your police department)
  • Mental Health Emergency? Please Dial: 988 (This will connect you to a crisis counselor who will talk to you.)
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) HelpLine Please Dial: 1-800-950-NAMI, or text “HELPLINE” to 62640. Available between 10 a.m.-10 p.m. ET, Monday–Friday
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline Please Dial: 1-800-799-7233
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255); Suicide Prevention Website
  • Crisis Text Line: Text REASON to 741741 (there is no charge for this, it's kept anonymous, and it's available 24 hours a day in both English and Spanish)
  • Self-Harm Hotline Please Dial: 1-800-DONT CUT (1-800-366-8288)
  • Family Violence Helpline Please Dial: 1-800-996-6228
  • Planned Parenthood Hotline Please Dial: 1-800-230-PLAN (7526)
  • National Council on Alcoholism & Drug Dependency: 1-800-622-2255
  • LGBTQ Hotline: 1-888-843-4564
  • Rape Abuse and Incest National Network (RAINN) Please Dial: 1(800) 656-HOPE / 1(800) 810-7440
  • The StrongHearts Native Helpline is a confidential and anonymous culturally appropriate domestic violence and dating violence helpline for Native Americans, available every day from 7 a.m.-10 p.m. CT. Call 1-844-762-8483.
